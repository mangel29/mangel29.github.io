Overview

Purpose

Nothing can compare to the thrill and fun of floating down some of the America's fastest flowing rivers. For everyone who wants to experience the best water in America but isn't sure if they're up for the challenge, this website should help them know that with Down By The River Rafting, they'll be in the best hands. Everything from the colors to the type to the layout of the website should be both build excitement and ensure security for visitors.

Audience

White water rafting is a favorite activity of the everyday outdoorsman and woman, but it is enjoyed by people of all backgrounds, including children ages 6+. The site's demographic will typically consist of individuals receiving middle to upper class income. Over 60% of annual rafters are caucasian males.
