The Shape Shifter Story

I always said I needed to eat better and exercise more, I hadn't felt very healthy for a while, but finding the time to do it seemed impossible. During a routine visit to the doctor, I was informed that if I didn't make some health changes myself, pretty soon some changes would be decided for me. That was the wake-up call I needed. Finding time was one challenge, but knowing how and what to eat and how and when to exercise was a whole other challenge entirely. I found whatever resources I could, wherever I could, and did my best to follow my new routine. With a lot of effort, the results came, but I couldn't help but think that all of this could be easier if there was one place to go for all of the information I had gathered. Shape Shifter was built to make something hard a little easier. It's not about how you look, every body is different, but it's about how you feel. We're here to offer our help, advice, and endless support as you make big changes to your health and life.
