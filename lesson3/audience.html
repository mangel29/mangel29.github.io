Target Audience

The target audience are men and women from teenage years to senior citizens. Weather or not will provide the audience with a free and easy way to prepare for changes in the weather, both severe and mild.

Personas

  • Health Enthusiast - Breanne(27), stays fit by eating right and jogging every morning. Her exercise routine depends on the weather.
  • Nature Lover - Mark (47), feels most at home outside in nature, hiking, camping, and breathing fresh air. He relies on good weather to enjoy the outdoors.

Scenarios

  • Before starting her daily run, Breanne first opens Weather or Not to check the weather, hoping that it's not too cold or rainy to workout outside.
  • Mark has been all over the place, but before choosing a new or old place to go, he always checks Weather or Not to be sure of where to go to best experience the outdoors.
  • Arthur likes to bike to work when the weather is good but he needs to get up early to get to work on time. Before going to bed, he checks Weather or Not to know if he should wake up early to ride or sleep in and drive.
  • Maria wants to plant some flowers in front of her home, she checks Weather or Not to make sure the weather will not be too bitter for the flowers.
  • Edward checks Weather or Not to know if his commute could be extended due to hazardous weather conditions.
